# AEO — Answer Engine Optimisation (Google AI Overviews, Perplexity)
1
FAQ pages with schema markup
Target questions users ask AI: "What is the average property price in Navi Mumbai?", "Is Navi Mumbai a good place to invest?", "Which areas in Navi Mumbai are developing fastest?" — answer each in 40–60 words, add FAQPage schema.

2
Structured "snippet bait" content
Format blogs with clear H2 questions + short direct paragraph answers. Google's AI Overview and Perplexity extract these. Example: H2 "How much does a 2BHK cost in Kharghar?" → one-paragraph answer with exact range.

3
Definition + comparison blocks
"What is RERA and how does it protect buyers in Navi Mumbai?" — definitional content gets featured snippets. Structure as definition box at top of article.

4
Numbered lists for process queries
"Steps to buy property in Navi Mumbai", "Documents needed for home loan in India" — numbered lists get position-zero placements and are cited in AI answers.
